# Credit Underwriter

<figure><img src="/files/3XT5ieGGVsf1n8GcdEET" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The 3Jane Credit Risk Algorithm (3CA) is a 3Jane-operated offchain credit underwriting algorithm that underwrites credit lines against Jane scores and assets, and derives the (1) credit line size, (2) default risk risk premium %, and (3) repayment rate.

* **Jane Score**: Composition of onchain & offchain creditworthiness, including Cred score, Blockchain Bureau score, and Equifax / TransUnion VantageScore 3.0 credit scores.
* **Assets**: DeFi assets, CEX crypto assets, and bank cash balances.
