# Silo Rage-Quit

The szipUSD vault is a Moloch v3 DAO, which means that there is a `ragequit` function, which allows for all share-holders to receive a pro-rata share of the remaining liquidity within the Vault.

A silo is shut down one withdrawal at a time, through the CoW orderbook. If there is a rush on liquidity, then the USDC within the Credit Warehouse will be pulled out to meet the needs of depositors, until no more supply is avaialble for creditors. 

At that point, all principle, yield, and actualized losses are split amongst the cohort, and the Credit Warehouse is closed.
