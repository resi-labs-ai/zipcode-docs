# Zipcode Miners are Curators

Bittensor provides a framework where miners compete for incentives, and each subnet has their own game.

As a Risk Curator, you are evaluiated on: 
1) $SN46 Buybacks vs. Incentives Spend
2) Average Duration Term & Default Risk.

Miners are scored in their performance on these two areas, and emissions are distributed according to these scores.

Zipcode welcomes competition amongst existing [DeFi Curators](https://defillama.com/protocols/risk-curators), and will offer our infrastsructure to TradFi funds who would benefit from operating an onchain credit warehouse.
