# Zipcode Miners are Curators

Bittensor is a competitive incentives mechanism designed to optimize toward a goal.

Curators on Zipcode have two goals:

1) Curators are graded on what their incentives spend is vs. their yield.
2) Curators are graded on their risk management, in the form of their Default Rate.

Miners are scored in their performance on these two areas, and emissions are distributed according to these scores.

Zipcode welcomes competition amongst existing [DeFi Curators](https://defillama.com/protocols/risk-curators), as well as private credit companies within TradFi, who want to build on top of an existing network / infrastructure.
