# Bittensor Credit Oracle

A Credit Oracle is a basket of information which validates Lines of Credit:

* **Plaid** pulls KYB & Business Credit Score.
* **Erebor** Enables ACH Bank Integration.
* **Proof** provides Notary access which validate Lien Custody & Repurchase Agreements.

APIs pull the information above, and validators on the Zipcode Subnet provide secure confirmation through zkProofs.

Chainlink CRE relays this datastream from Bittensor to EVM, and enables gated access &  to [Euler Vaults](/backing/warehouse).
