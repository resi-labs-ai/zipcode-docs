# Bittensor Credit Oracle

A Credit Oracle is a basket of information which indicates that a credit line has been validated, and for what amount: 

* **Plaid** pulls KYB and Business Credit Score.
* **Erebor** faciliates Bank Integration.
* **Proof** provides Notary access which validate Lien Custody & Repurchase Agreements.

Zipcode works backward from the underwriting qualifications secondary market partners, to ensure that all credit lines have a Take-Out provider. Underwriting terms are set by secondary markets, and then the APIs above pull the information. 

### How does that information get validated, and pushed onchain? 

* **A Bittensor subnet runs zkProof Validation.** Data is confirmed and packaged into a customer account, without exposing underlying personal information. 
* **Chainlink CRE relays this information Onchain.** CRE is a runtime environment that automates and transmites information onchain. CRE provides the foundation for the [gated access to Euler Vaults](/backing/warehouse) as well as the lien value the protocol underwrites against.

These requirements must be met before any credit line is opened.
