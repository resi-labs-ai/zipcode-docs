# Asset-Backed Credit.

* Private Credit must have collateral, and legal recourse.
* Collateral is only as real as its secondary market bid.
* Value must programmatically accrue to the token. 

These are the core DeFi principles that we used when designing Zipcode.

### Zipcode is a private credit rail that funds HELOC originators:

1) The Zipcode Network pays USDC depositors to pool capital. 
2) The Zipcode Credit Oracle underwrites originators who already have established relationships with secondary takeout markets.
3) Each line of credit is collateralized by a lien, held in SPV custody with a repurchase agreement.
4) The originator repurchases the lien, and pays off the loan within 90 days, or Zipcode claims the lien.
5) Yields flow back into the Zipcode Network through programmatic buybacks.
