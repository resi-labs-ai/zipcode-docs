# Automation & Oracles with Chainlink CRE

Chainlink CRE transmits sources of truth, and runs operations for Zipcode. 

The [Bittensor Credit Oracle](/bank-of-bittensor/credit-oracle) fetches and zk-verifies account & underwriting data, and those findings are transmitted to by Chainlink CRE.

1) Who is this borrower? 
2) Does this borrower qualify for access to this Credit Line? 
3) What bank account do they hold to receive funds, and repay? 
4) How much are they qualified to borrow, given the value of their lien?

This information needs to pass through CRE to unlock Euler Vaults, set Oracle Rates, and fulfill / repay credit lines.

Chainlink CRE enables autonomous actions within a narrow scope of behavor, and those actions are validated by an immutable Forwarder, written in Go.

### Gnosis Safe Zodiac Modules & Roles v2

Gnosis Safe allows for arbitrary calls to be made within Safes, which means that if you are a treasury, you can make any custom call you like onchain through Safe. 

Vaults can make use of this infrastructure through Zodiac Modules & Roles v2. 

* Zodiac Modules are signers on a Safe which are given permission to take specifically constrained onchain actions. 
* Roles v2 designate parties that are allowed to trigger those actions. 

In Zipcode, CRE workflows are assigned roles, so they can execute actions constrained onchain actions on behalf of the Vault. 

### The CRE family

Zipcode operates with a family of CRE workflows, each owning exactly one sensitive operation:

* **[Credit Oracle](/bank-of-bittensor/credit-oracle)** — Verification through the `ZipcodeController`, and Lien Oracle operations through the `ZipcodeOracleRegistry`.
* **Warehouse Operations** — Allocations and withdrawals of Lines of Credit. Limited to four pre-scoped operations, with no custody.
* **Duration Risk Coordinator** — Handling Default Risk through `DefaultCoordinator`, and processing the protocol's exit queue through the CoW orderbook.
* **Yield Strategist** — a Protocol Owned Liquidity Strategy for a zipUSD/ZIPCODE LP pool on Hydrex. Boosts yield for the szipUSD vault, and decreases emissions sell pressure.
