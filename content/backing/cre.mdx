# Automation & Oracles with Chainlink CRE

Chainlink CRE transmits verified truthes, and runs operations for Zipcode. 

The [Bittensor Credit Oracle](/bank-of-bittensor/credit-oracle) fetches and zk-verifies account & underwriting data, and passes that data to Chainlink CRE.

1) Who is the borrower? 
2) What credit lines do they qualify for? 
3) What account will they use to receive funds, and repay?
4) What is the value of their Lien? 
5) How much can they borrow? 

Chainlink CRE enables these checks to take place & runs operations on the warehouse.

### Gnosis Safe Zodiac Modules & Roles v2

Did you know that Safes make great Vault Infrastructure? 

* Arbitrary calls can be made to run any strategy you like. 
* Zodiac Modules are signers, which have been given permission to take narrow onchain actions.
* Roles v2 designate parties that are allowed to trigger those onchain actions. 

Chainlink CRE is the ideal operations layer for the Zodiac vault stack.

### CRE Workflows

Each of the following CRE workflows owns a separate vault operation:

* **Credit Oracle** — Verification through the `ZipcodeController`, and Lien Oracle operations through the `ZipcodeOracleRegistry`.
* **Warehouse Operations** — Allocations and withdrawals of Lines of Credit.
* **Duration Risk Coordinator** — Managing Default Risk through `DefaultCoordinator` & processing the protocol's exit queue through the CoW orderbook.
* **Yield Strategist** — a Protocol Owned Liquidity Strategy for a zipUSD/ZIPCODE LP, which boosts yield and decreases emissions sell pressure. 

[CRE workflows are still under Audit.]
